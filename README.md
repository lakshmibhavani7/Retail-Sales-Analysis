## Project Overview

Analyzed retail sales data using SQL, Python, and Power BI to evaluate sales performance, product profitability, and regional trends to support data-driven business decisions.

- Total sales tracked: **820K**
- Total profit analyzed: **260K**
- Total marketing spend: **132K**
- Top performing state: **California** (31,785 profit)
- Top selling product: **Columbian** (128K sales)
- Highest revenue region: **West** (710K)

## Key Steps

**1. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing (Python)**
- Handled missing values and incorrect data types
- Converted dates to Year and Month for trend analysis
- Verified consistency across product, category, and region data

**2. SQL Analysis**
- Created Master Table by joining Fact, Product, and Location tables
- Identified California as top performing state (31,785 profit)
- Analyzed top products, regional sales, and marketing spend efficiency
- Calculated KPIs — total sales, total profit, margin, and inventory metrics

**3. Dashboard Visualization (Power BI)**
- KPI cards for total sales (820K), total profit (260K), and marketing spend (132K)
- Sales and Profit by Product Type and Category
- Marketing Spend vs Profit by Product and State
- Top Performing States by Profit
- Monthly sales trends across 2015 to 2018
- Slicers for Product Type, Category, State, Segment, and Region

## Key Insights

- West region generates highest total revenue (710K)
- Technology category contributes most to overall revenue
- California is the top performing state with 31,785 profit
- Columbian is the top selling product with 128K in sales
- Consistent seasonal peaks observed across 2015 to 2018
- High marketing spend does not always correlate with high profit

## Business Impact

- Enables data driven product and marketing decisions
- Optimizes sales strategies by region and product type
- Supports seasonal inventory planning based on historical trends
- Identifies underperforming states and products for improvement
