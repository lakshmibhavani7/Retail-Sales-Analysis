# Retail & Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL and Python to evaluate sales performance, product profitability, and regional trends. The goal is to extract actionable business insights and provide visualizations for strategic decision-making.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python:** Data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, trend analysis (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- **SQL:** Data aggregation, joins, master table creation, advanced queries  
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard for visualization with KPIs and slicers  

---

## Folder Structure
Data/ - Contains MasterTable_SQL.csv and SalesDataset.csv
Python/ - Jupyter notebooks for EDA and analysis
SQL/ - SQL queries for master table and business insights
PowerBI/ - Power BI dashboard file

---

## Key Steps

### Data Cleaning & Preprocessing (Python)
- Handled missing values and incorrect data types in sales dataset  
- Converted dates to Year & Month for trend analysis  
- Verified consistency across product, category, and region data  

### SQL Analysis
- Created **Master Table** by joining Fact, Product, and Location tables  
- Generated business-focused queries for insights like:
  - Top-performing products  
  - Profit and sales by state  
  - Marketing spend efficiency  
  - Loss-making products or areas  
- Calculated KPIs: total sales, total profit, margin, and inventory metrics  

### Dashboard Visualization (Power BI)
- KPI cards for total sales, total profit, total marketing spend, product count  
- Charts:
  - Sales & Profit by Product Type & Category  
  - Marketing Spend vs Profit by Product and State  
  - Top Performing States by Profit  
  - Monthly sales trends  
  - Top products by revenue  
- Slicers for **Product_Type, Product_Category, State, Segment, Region**  

---

## Insights & Recommendations
- **West region** generates the highest total sales  
- **Technology category** contributes most to overall revenue  
- **Seasonal peaks** observed in monthly sales trends  
- Top products contribute a large share of total revenue  
- Focus marketing on **high-margin products** to maximize ROI  
- Monitor underperforming states/products for improvement  
- Leverage seasonal trends for inventory planning and promotions  

---

## Business Impact
These insights help businesses:  
- Make data-driven product and marketing decisions  
- Optimize sales strategies by region and product type  
- Plan campaigns efficiently based on historical performance
